Tutorial for simulation study and application to Alzeheimer's disease in the paper
“The Underlap Coefficient as Measure of a Biomarker’s Discriminatory
Ability”
================
Zhaoxi Zhang, Vanda Inácio, and Miguel de Carvalho

In this tutorial we describe the steps for obtaining the results of the
simulation study and application to Alzeheimer's disease in Section 4 and 5 of the paper.

The tutorials of how to get the plots in Section 4 and 5 of the paper are in the "tutorials" folder of the repository. The functions needed for the simulation section and the application section are in the "functions" folder of the repository.
